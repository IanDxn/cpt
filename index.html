This page will allow you to obtain the hashed password that would be generated automatically by the PwdHash browser extension. The process of generating the hashed password is performed locally on your machine, without sending any information to Stanford.

When entering site address, you may cut and paste the full URL or simply enter the site name (e.g. "www.stanford.edu") or domain name ("stanford.edu"). Only the domain name is actually used.

If you are using the PwdHash extension right now to view this page, you may experience a double hashing, resulting in an unusable password. You can get around this problem by omitting the password prefix (@@) part of your password, but don't get in the habit of doing this at other sites!
